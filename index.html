Introduction:

Welcome to our Wordle Game! Challenge your mind and guess the hidden word.



Instructions:

You need to guess the hidden word.
You have a limited number of attempts to get it right.
After each guess, the letters will change color:
Green = correct letter in the correct position
Yellow = correct letter but wrong position
Red = letter is not in the word
Use the clues to improve your next guess.



To start the game, please enter your username.
